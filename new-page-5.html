About

Xecuit.partners is the acquisition and strategic partnership division of Xecuit LLC. We are firmly focused on acquiring and partnering with businesses that can benefit from technology solutions that can grow their bottom line. We work with our partners to identify their most critical challenges, offer expert advice, and deliver real-world solutions that will transform and grow businesses. We combine subject matter expertise, industry experience, and technology solutions developed internally to improve companies within our portfolio.

Xecuit.partners is focused on building a highly select number of companies into enterprises with global scale and performance capabilities through process improvement, infusion of technology, and innovation. 

Business representatives of companies that fit our criteria are encouraged to contact us for a completely confidential exploratory discussion of the business and what growth opportunities may be available through partnership or acquisition.

Easiest Way to Build Sites

What type of business does xecuit acquire?

■ Businesses with a proven management team or seller retention post-acquisition

■ We acquire hardware, software and services businesses across most industries

■ Niche, non-commoditized product line or service offering

■ Businesses that can benefit from an infusion or improvement of technology

■ Brick and mortar businesses with a strong local presence

■ We look for high growth potential and market traction

■ We are currently pursuing opportunities in North America

What size of acquisitions is xecuit interested in?

We look to acquire profitable companies with revenue ranging from $500k up to more mature firms with revenues of $50MM+.

How do acquired businesses fit into xecuit portfolio?

■ Standalone or platform acquisitions which expand Xecuit’s portfolio

■ “Bolt-on” acquisitions which bring additional capabilities or have synergies with one or more of our existing portfolio companies

Why Xecuit?

■ We have a deep understanding of technology

■ Maintaining company heritage, staff, culture and customer, sector, and technology focus are at the core of our acquisition approach.

■ Our team is experienced and can execute transactions quickly, efficiently and sensitively.
