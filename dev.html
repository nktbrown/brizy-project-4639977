xecuit

Xecuit is a design-centric minority-owned and operated web & mobile app development firm based in NJ.  We work with nonprofits, government agencies, and businesses, to design and develop digital solutions that increase growth, visibility and business efficiency. Our unique flat-rate pricing gives clients confidence that projects are delivered on budget and within scope.

Featured Case Study

Skill Scout is a video production company's who offers a DIY video production offering where clients capture footage with their cell phone and their team collects the footage and edits it into a final product. They had an outdated web platform that didn't offer the best user experience and constantly resulted in client and employee frustration with the upload process and over user experience.


We collaborated with Skill Scout to design a client facing mobile app that allows clients to easily capture organize notate footage and seamlessly upload that footage into a web based platform for their editors to navigate and tackle projects.
