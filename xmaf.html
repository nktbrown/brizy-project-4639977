Xecuit Minority

Acquisition Fund

The Xecuit Minority Acquisition Fund (XMAF) is a minority operated fund dedicated to acquiring businesses that enable us to create impact among the communities that we as African American entrepreneurs represent and identify with. Through this fund we plan to acquire businesses that enable us to train young entrepreneurs, create job for people from disenfranchised communities, and make various charitable contributions from the profits of the businesses . Donations into this fund earns you a seat on the fund's Advisory Council which gives you a voice in selected areas of our investments.
